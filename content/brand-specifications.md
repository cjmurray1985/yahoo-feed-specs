Establishing a strong brand presence on Yahoo is essential for conveying expertise and fostering audience trust. The Brand Page serves as the central hub for your brand identity, aggregating your content and providing a unified destination for your audience on Yahoo.

**Submission Process:** Please submit the assets listed below to your Yahoo Partner Manager for implementation.

## **Brand Page Assets**

### **Cover Image**

Defines the visual identity of your brand page.

* **Dimensions:** 2560 px width × 440 px height
* **Format:** JPEG or PNG (Horizontal orientation recommended)

### **Brand Logo**

Partners are required to provide **4 company logo assets** in JPEG or PNG format to ensure proper rendering across all devices and themes.

**Required Assets:**

1. **Horizontal Logo (Light Theme):** Minimum 300px height. Used primarily on light-themed pages. Preferably a single line.
2. **Horizontal Logo (Dark Theme):** Minimum 300px height. Used on dark-themed pages.
3. **Horizontal Logo (White):** Minimum 300px height. "All white" version used for rare cases with full-color backgrounds.
4. **Circle Logo:** Minimum 400px × 400px. Displayed prominently at the top of your brand page.

**Notes:**

* All horizontal logos (Light, Dark, White) must have a **transparent background**.
* Our design team will review and modify sizes if necessary for fit and feel on Yahoo.

### **Brand Description**

A concise summary of your brand's niche and topical authority.

* **Length:** 250–500 characters

## **Engagement & Social**

### **Link to Website**

Will appear at the top of your brand page.

* **Restriction:** Links to commerce or affiliate sites are not permitted.

### **Social Links**

Displayed on your brand page and content. Please provide URLs for at least two of the following:

* TikTok (Embed)
* Instagram (Embed)
* Facebook
* Medium
* Email
* Pinterest
* X/Twitter
* YouTube
